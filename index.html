RED BUS

redBus is a popular online platform for booking bus tickets, particularly in India, and has expanded its services to include hotel bookings. It was founded in 2006 by a group of engineers and is now part of MakeMyTrip Limited.

redBus aims to provide a convenient and reliable way for people to book bus travel, connecting them with various bus operators and offering a range of options and deals.

TNSTC

TNSTC is a government-owned public transport corporation operating in Tamil Nadu. It provides affordable and accessible bus services across urban and rural areas of the state. TNSTC operates intercity, intracity, and long-distance routes to support daily commuters and travelers. It maintains a large fleet of buses including ordinary, express, and deluxe services. TNSTC plays a vital role in promoting connectivity and regional development across Tamil Nadu.

SETC

SETC is a premium government bus service under the Tamil Nadu State Transport Department. It operates long-distance express and luxury bus services between major cities and states. SETC buses offer comfortable travel with facilities like air-conditioning and pushback seats. It plays a key role in connecting Tamil Nadu with neighboring states like Karnataka and Kerala.
